About

Hi, I'm Virginia Lee

I'm a passionate music educator with over a decade of teaching experience. My approach blends classical technique with the music students actually love — because inspiration is the most powerful teaching tool of all.

Whether you're sitting at a piano for the very first time or preparing for an audition, I tailor every lesson to you: your goals, your pace, and your unique musical voice.
