ISNS All-Staff Meeting · Sept 10, 2025

Mirrors, Windows, and Sliding Glass Doors: DEIJB Staff Training 2025

Click to expand

As chair of the ISNS DEIJB Committee, I developed this all-staff training to introduce a framework that has transformed how I think about the materials and experiences we design for students. Drawing on the theory of Mirrors, Windows, and Sliding Glass Doors — introduced by Emily Styles and expanded by Dr. Rudine Sims Bishop — I challenge educators to ask: do our students see themselves in what we teach? Are they encountering new perspectives? Are they empowered to act? Grounded in community survey data, this session gives staff a shared lens for making their curriculum more inclusive, expansive, and meaningful for every learner.
